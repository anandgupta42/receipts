the samosa

“Buy me a samosa” is this project's tip jar — a nod to the maintainer's favorite snack. The receipt card itself stays clean; the invitation lives on pages like this one.

Unicode ships no samosa emoji. Searching U+1F300–U+1FAFF for SAMOSA or PASTRY returns nothing; the nearest matches are DUMPLING (U+1F95F) and PIE. For a tool whose entire premise is that every character on a receipt is true, shipping a dumpling labeled “samosa” was the one mislabel we couldn't keep.

And this product does not print approximations: the receipt prints no stand-in emoji at all — where a samosa appears (this site, the PR artifact page), it is the real thing: the hand-authored outline above.
