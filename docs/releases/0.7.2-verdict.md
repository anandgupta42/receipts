  aireceipts-format refs existed; every `refs/receipts/*` ref was a foreign in-toto
  attestation. CI `pr-check` log on altimate-backend: `invalid receipt payload ‚Äî
  schemaVersion: expected 1; treating as missing receipt` ‚Üí `missing-notice`. The auto-attach
  hook fired (170 refs in altimate-backend) but no receipt ever posted.

  shows nothing under `refs/receipts/`.
- **Regression-pinned:** `test/pr/store.test.ts` asserts `RECEIPT_REF_PREFIX ===
  "refs/aireceipts/"`; `test/pr/gitWrite.test.ts` covers the new `refs/aireceipts/*` push.
